## Template Usage Guidelines

### Personalization
- Always use the customer's name
- Reference specific details from their inquiry
- Include relevant account information
- Customize based on their plan or service level

### Tone and Style
- Professional yet friendly
- Empathetic when appropriate
- Clear and concise
- Action-oriented

### Response Time
- Acknowledge receipt within 2 hours
- Provide full response within SLA timeframe
- Follow up as promised
- Escalate if needed

### Quality Checks
- Proofread for grammar and spelling
- Verify all links work correctly
- Ensure accuracy of information
- Confirm next steps are clear
